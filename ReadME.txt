545 is 5 Right Answers for5 Grades :)

it is a educational game for kids to learn them 
Mathmatics by solving some equations (addition-Subtraction-Multplication-Division and modulas)

You win if you get 5 right answers .

you lose if your life count became Zero.

when you answer 5 wrong answers your life count will decreament.

to end the game you can press ESC at any time .

for simplification all equations were choosen so that the result 
for any operation will be only one number between 0 and 10.

Enjoy, play and learn .
